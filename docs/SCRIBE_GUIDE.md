## Quick Start (2 Minutes)

### Step 1: Sign Up
1. Go to **hanna.care/scribe/app**
2. Enter your email (use your hospital email)
3. Create a 6-digit PIN (memorable, you'll use this daily)
4. Enter your display name (e.g., "Dr. Somchai")
5. Tap **Create Account**

### Step 2: Record Your First Note
1. Tap the **+ button** (bottom right)
2. Enter patient details:
   - **Patient Name**: Full name
   - **HN**: Hospital number (optional)
   - **Template**: SOAP (default) or Progress Note
3. Tap **Start Recording**
4. Tap the **orb** to begin recording
5. Speak naturally (3-5 minutes typical)
6. Tap **Done** when finished

### Step 3: Review & Finalize
1. Wait 30-60 seconds for AI processing
2. Review each SOAP section
3. Edit if needed (tap any section)
4. Use **Hanna AI commands** for quick edits
5. Tap **Finalize** when ready
6. **Copy** or **Export PDF**

## Core Features

### 1. Recording Patient Encounters

#### The Orb Interface
- **Pulsing Blue Orb**: Ready to record
- **Solid Red Dot**: Currently recording
- **Pause Button**: Temporarily stop (e.g., sensitive discussion)
- **Resume Button**: Continue recording
- **Duration Timer**: Shows recording length

#### Best Practices
| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Speak naturally, conversationally | Read from a script |
| Include patient's own words | Use abbreviations without context |
| Mention relevant history | Rush through important details |
| Pause for sensitive topics | Record in noisy environments |
| Keep 30cm from mouth | Handle phone while recording |

#### Recording Tips
- **Ideal length**: 2-5 minutes
- **Maximum length**: 15 minutes (app will warn)
- **File size**: ~1MB per minute
- **Offline mode**: Not supported (requires internet)

### 2. AI Note Generation

#### What Happens After Recording
```
Recording (WebM audio)
    ↓ (10-20 seconds)
OpenAI Whisper Transcription
    ↓ (15-30 seconds)
Llama 3.3 70B Clinical Analysis
    ↓ (5-10 seconds)
Structured SOAP Note
    ↓
Ready for Review
```

**Total processing time**: 30-60 seconds typical

#### SOAP Structure
| Section | What It Contains |
|---------|------------------|
| **Subjective** | Patient's story, symptoms, history, review of systems |
| **Objective** | Vitals, physical exam findings, lab results |
| **Assessment** | Diagnosis, differential diagnosis, clinical reasoning |
| **Plan** | Treatment, medications, follow-up, patient education |

#### Language Support
- **Thai**: Full support (transcription + generation)
- **English**: Full support
- **Mixed (Thai + English)**: Supported (medical terms often in English)

### 3. AI-Powered Editing

#### Section Regeneration
Regenerate any SOAP section with AI:

1. Tap **Regenerate** button next to any section
2. (Optional) Add instruction:
   - *"Add differential diagnosis"*
   - *"Include medication dosages"*
   - *"Make more detailed"*
   - *"Summarize briefly"*
3. Wait 10-15 seconds
4. Review new content
5. Keep or regenerate again

#### Hanna Commands (Natural Language Editing)
At the bottom of the editor, use the **Hanna AI command bar**:

**Examples**:
```
"Make this more concise"
"Add red flag symptoms to rule out"
"Include patient education points"
"Expand on the physical examination"
"Add ICD-10 code suggestions"
"Translate to Thai"
"Translate to English"
"Format as a referral letter"
```

**How to Use**:
1. Type your command in the input field
2. Press **Enter** or tap **Apply**
3. Wait 10-20 seconds
4. Review changes across all sections

### 4. Export & Share

#### Copy to Clipboard
1. Tap **Copy** button
2. Note content copied as plain text
3. Paste into your EMR system

**Format**:
```
Patient: John Doe
HN: HN12345
Date: 2026-03-04 14:30
Type: SOAP
Status: Finalized

SUBJECTIVE
[Content]

OBJECTIVE
[Content]

ASSESSMENT
[Content]

PLAN
[Content]
```

#### PDF Export
1. Tap **PDF** button
2. Downloads formatted PDF note
3. Includes:
   - Clinic header (coming soon)
   - Patient demographics
   - Full SOAP content
   - Timestamp and provider name
   - "Generated by Hanna Scribe" footer

**PDF Use Cases**:
- Patient records
- Referral letters
- Insurance documentation
- Legal documentation

#### Finalize vs. Draft
| Status | Meaning | Can Edit? |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Draft** | AI-generated, not yet reviewed | Yes, fully editable |
| **Finalized** | Clinician-reviewed and approved | Yes, but requires amendment |

**Best Practice**: Always finalize notes at the end of each clinic session.

### 5. Handover Summaries

#### What is Handover?
Automatic shift handover report generated from your day's notes.

#### How to Generate
1. Tap **Handover** tab (bottom navigation)
2. Review auto-generated summary:
   - Total patients seen
   - Urgent cases flagged
   - Brief summary per patient
3. Tap **Copy** or **Export PDF**
4. Share with on-call team

#### Example Handover

## Troubleshooting

### Recording Issues

#### "Microphone access denied"
**Fix**: 
1. Go to browser settings
2. Allow microphone for hanna.care
3. Refresh page
4. Try again

#### "Recording keeps stopping"
**Possible causes**:
- Poor internet connection
- Browser memory full
- Background app interference

**Fix**:
1. Check internet (WiFi or 4G+)
2. Close other tabs/apps
3. Restart browser
4. Try shorter recordings first

#### "Audio quality is poor"
**Tips**:
- Hold phone 20-30cm from mouth
- Speak clearly, not too fast
- Avoid noisy environments
- Use quiet room when possible

---

### Processing Issues

#### "Transcription failed"
**Common causes**:
- Audio file corrupted
- Server timeout
- Unsupported audio format

**Fix**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Try recording again (shorter)
3. Contact support if persists

#### "AI generation taking too long"
**Normal time**: 30-60 seconds  
**Slow time**: 2-3 minutes (peak hours)

**If >3 minutes**:
1. Refresh page
2. Check "Notes" tab (may have completed)
3. Contact support

#### "Generated note is inaccurate"
**Why this happens**:
- Poor audio quality
- Heavy accent or mumbling
- Medical jargon not recognized
- Background noise interference

**Fix**:
1. Use **Regenerate** with instruction
2. Manually edit sections
3. Use **Hanna Commands** to refine
4. Re-record if necessary (delete session)

## Privacy & Security

### Data Protection
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- **Storage**: Supabase PostgreSQL (Thai data centers)
- **Access**: PIN-based authentication
- **Audit logs**: All actions logged

### What We Store
| Data Type | Stored? | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Audio recordings | Yes | 30 days |
| Transcripts | Yes | Indefinite |
| Generated notes | Yes | Indefinite |
| User email | Yes | Account lifetime |
| Payment info | No (Stripe only) | Per Stripe policy |

### What We Don't Store
- ❌ Raw audio after 30 days
- ❌ Credit card numbers
- ❌ Patient identifiers beyond name/HN
- ❌ Location data

### Data Export
You can export all your data anytime:
1. Settings → Export Data
2. Receive JSON file with all notes
3. Includes transcripts and metadata

### Account Deletion
1. Settings → Delete Account
2. Confirm deletion
3. All data deleted within 30 days
4. Cannot be undone

### Common Use Cases

#### Primary Care Clinic
**Typical day**: 25-30 patients  
**Time per note**: 3-5 minutes recording, 1 minute review  
**Time saved**: 4-6 hours/day

**Workflow**:
1. Patient enters
2. Start recording (discreetly on desk)
3. Natural consultation
4. Tap done, patient waits
5. Quick review while patient dresses
6. Finalize, give patient copy

#### Specialist Clinic
**Typical day**: 15-20 patients  
**Time per note**: 5-8 minutes recording, 2 minutes review  
**Time saved**: 2-3 hours/day

**Workflow**:
1. Record detailed history
2. Include physical exam findings
3. Dictate assessment reasoning
4. Generate comprehensive note
5. Edit complex cases
6. Finalize end of day

#### Emergency Department
**Typical shift**: 10-15 patients  
**Time per note**: 2-4 minutes recording, 1 minute review  
**Time saved**: 1-2 hours/shift

**Workflow**:
1. Quick recording at bedside
2. Focus on chief complaint + decision making
3. Generate note during next patient
4. Review between patients
5. Handover at shift end

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use Hanna Scribe offline?**  
A: No, internet connection required for transcription and AI generation.

**Q: Does it work with thick accents?**  
A: Yes, Whisper AI handles accents well. Speak clearly for best results.

**Q: Can I edit notes after finalizing?**  
A: Yes, tap "Amend" to make changes. Finalized status is retained.

**Q: Is my data secure?**  
A: Yes, encrypted end-to-end. We're PDPA-compliant and HIPAA-aligned.

**Q: Can I export to my EMR?**  
A: Currently copy-paste. HL7/FHIR integration coming Q2 2026.

**Q: What if I exceed my plan limit?**  
A: Free plan: blocked after 10 notes. Pro/Clinic: unlimited.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Yes, pro-rated refund for unused subscription time.

**Q: Does it work on iPhone and Android?**  
A: Yes, works on any smartphone with modern browser (Chrome, Safari).

**Q: Can multiple providers share one account?**  
A: Technically yes, but against ToS. Each provider needs own account (Clinic plan covers up to 5).
