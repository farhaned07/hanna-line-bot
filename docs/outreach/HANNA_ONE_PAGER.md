# Hanna: 10x Nurse Capacity through Supervised AI

---

## The Problem

Thailand faces a shortage of **35,000 nurses**. Hospitals cannot hire their way out.

The result:
- Burnout and turnover
- Missed follow-ups between clinic visits
- Preventable readmissions and complications

---

---

## Regulatory Posture

Hanna is **infrastructure**, not a practitioner.

- All clinical decisions remain with licensed staff
- Patients are informed they are interacting with an AI assistant
- Emergency keywords trigger immediate human escalation (1669 referral)
