# Patient Guide (English Summary)

## What is Hanna?

Hanna is your AI health assistant. It helps your care team monitor your health every day.

**Hanna will:**
- Send daily health check-ins
- Remind you about medications
- Listen to your symptoms
- Alert your nurse if something seems wrong

**Hanna will NOT:**
- Give medical advice
- Prescribe medications
- Diagnose conditions

**Important:** Hanna is not a doctor or nurse. In an emergency, call **1669** immediately.

## How to Use

1. Add Hanna on LINE (@hanna)
2. Answer simple daily questions (yes/no)
3. Report any symptoms you have

## Emergency

If you experience chest pain, difficulty breathing, or fainting, call **1669** immediately.
