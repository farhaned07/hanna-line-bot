## Week 2: Patient Enrollment

### Cohort Selection

**Recommended criteria:**

| Criteria | Recommendation |
|----------|----------------|
| **Conditions** | Diabetes Type 2, Hypertension (or both) |
| **Size** | 500-1,000 patients |
| **Age** | 40-80 years |
| **Tech readiness** | Uses LINE regularly |
| **Language** | Thai speakers |

**Exclusion criteria:**
- Patients in acute care
- Patients who don't use smartphones
- Non-Thai speakers (current limitation)

### Enrollment Process

1. **Prepare patient list** using provided template
2. **Review privacy checklist** (PDPA compliance)
3. **Submit data** via secure upload
4. **System configuration** (Hanna team, 24-48 hours)
5. **Enrollment confirmation** sent to you

### Patient Communication

We recommend informing patients about Hanna before go-live:

> "เราขอแนะนำ Hanna ผู้ช่วยดูแลสุขภาพ AI ที่จะช่วยติดตามสุขภาพของคุณทุกวัน Hanna จะส่งข้อความถามสุขภาพและเตือนเรื่องยา ถ้ามีอะไรผิดปกติ พยาบาลจะติดต่อคุณทันที"
>
> (We're introducing Hanna, an AI health assistant that will help monitor your health daily. Hanna will message you about your health and remind you about medications. If anything seems wrong, a nurse will contact you immediately.)

Hanna sends welcome messages to enrolled patients:

### Your Role

| Activity | Frequency |
|----------|-----------|
| Monitor dashboard | Daily (15-30 min) |
| Respond to escalations | As they arise |

| Log any issues | As they occur |

### Common Week 1 Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Patient doesn't respond | Normal. Engagement ramps up over 2 weeks. |
| Patient confused | Send them the Patient Guide. |
| False positive alert | Resolve as "False Alarm" → Improves system. |
| Technical issue | Contact your Implementation Manager. |

## Week 5-10: Ongoing Operations

| Time | Activity |

1. Review engagement metrics
2. Discuss any challenges

## FAQ

**Q: What if a patient asks Hanna a medical question?**  
A: Hanna will redirect them to speak with their care team. Hanna does not provide medical advice.

**Q: What if a patient reports an emergency?**  
A: Emergency keywords trigger immediate 1669 referral and CRITICAL nurse alert.

**Q: How do we add new patients mid-pilot?**  
A: Contact your Implementation Manager. We can add patients in batches.

**Q: What happens to patient data after the pilot?**  
A: All data is retained for your records. If you don't continue, we securely delete after 30 days.
